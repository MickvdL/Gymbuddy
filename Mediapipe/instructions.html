Hammer curl Biceps, brachialis, brachioradialis
How to Perform
1
Grab a pair of dumbbells off the rack
2
Pull shoulders back and down, back straight, core tight
3
Arms hang at your sides, palms facing each other
4
Curl the dumbbells upward, elbows stay close to your body, squeeze at the top
5
Lower slowly in 2 to 3 seconds — don't let them drop
